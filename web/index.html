OUR STORY
Two Worlds. One Vision.

It started with an argument, the kind that never really ends. Two people, one couple, two completely different ways of seeing the world. One of us lives for the raw, electric, psychedelic side of things, acid colors, trippy patterns, the kind of designs that make you look twice. The other? Drawn to the warmth of vintage, the texture of classic, the beauty of things that feel timeless.

We tried to agree. We really did. But every sketch, every fabric choice, every color palette turned into a creative tug-of-war. And then it hit us: why choose?

What if we didn't compromise? What if instead of meeting in the middle, we built two entirely separate universes, each one pure, unfiltered, unapologetically itself?

That's how Out of Sight was born. In 2025, with no money, no experience, and nothing but a stubborn belief that the things you carry should say something about who you are.

We had to learn everything from zero. How to design. How to source materials. How to stitch. How to build a website. How to turn an idea that lived in our heads into something real that people could hold in their hands.

There were late nights, failed prototypes, arguments about thread colors at 2 AM, and moments where we almost gave up. But we didn't. Because every time we looked at what we were building, it felt right.

Today, Out of Sight is two worlds living under one roof. Two universes you can travel between with a single click. Out of Mind for those who want to stand out, trip out, and never blend in. Out of Era for those who carry the soul of another time.

Every backpack, every patch, every piece we make is designed for people who refuse to look like everyone else. We're not a big company. We don't have investors or a warehouse full of stock. We're two people who turned a difference into a brand, and a brand into something we're proud of.

Welcome to Out of Sight. Pick your universe. Or travel between both, we won't judge.
